About T1

T1 is a leading esports organization based in South Korea, renowned for its outstanding achievements in League of Legends. Established in 2003 under the name SK Telecom T1, the team quickly rose to become one of the strongest in the world.

With multiple world championship titles, T1 has demonstrated exceptional strength and skill in the esports scene. The team is known for its professional management, advanced training system, and talented roster. Many T1 players have become legends, known and admired by fans worldwide.

Currently, the CEO of T1 is Joe Marsh, who has held the position since 2019. With a strategic vision and strong leadership, Joe Marsh has guided T1's expansion and development not only in League of Legends but also in other games such as Valorant and Fortnite. Under his management, T1 has continued to grow and solidify its status as a symbol in the global esports industry.

T1 not only stands out for its competitive success but also focuses on building a strong and friendly fan community. With the slogan "T1 Fighting!", the team always receives enthusiastic support from fans around the world. Committed to delivering top-notch performances and continually striving for excellence, T1 remains one of the most formidable and respected teams in esports history.

Our Journey

2004: Foundation

T1 was established in 2004, marking the beginning of a new era in esports. With a vision to become a leader in the industry, T1 quickly made a name for itself in the competitive gaming world.

2013: First World Championship

In 2013, T1 won its first League of Legends World Championship, solidifying its status as a global powerhouse. The victory was a testament to the team's skill and dedication.

2015-2016: Continued Success

During this period, T1 continued to dominate the esports scene, winning back-to-back World Championships in 2015 and 2016. The team's unparalleled performances set new standards in the industry.

2019: Expanding to New Games

In 2019, T1 expanded its horizons by entering new games like Valorant and Fortnite. This move diversified the team's portfolio and showcased its versatility in the esports industry.

2024: Future Aspirations

As T1 continues to grow, the team remains committed to achieving new heights. With a strong foundation and a talented roster, T1 is poised to make history once again in the years to come.
